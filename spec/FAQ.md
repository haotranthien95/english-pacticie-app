## General Questions

### What is the English Practice App?
The English Practice App is a mobile application designed to help users improve their English language skills through interactive lessons, exercises, and personalized learning paths.

### Who is this app for?
The app is designed for English learners of all levels (Beginner to Advanced) who want to improve their vocabulary, grammar, reading, listening, speaking, and writing skills.

## User Experience Questions

### How will progress be tracked?
Users will have access to:
- Overall proficiency level
- Skill-specific progress (vocabulary, grammar, etc.)
- Completed lessons and exercises
- Time spent learning
- Streak tracking
- Achievement badges

### Can users customize their learning path?
Yes, while the app provides recommended learning paths based on proficiency assessment, users will be able to:
- Set personal learning goals
- Choose specific topics to focus on
- Adjust difficulty levels
- Select preferred exercise types

### Will there be social features?
Social features such as community forums, language exchange partners, and shared progress are planned for Phase 3. The MVP will focus on individual learning.
